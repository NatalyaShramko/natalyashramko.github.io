The interaction with the product begins with feeling and reaction as a consequence of using it. The important ones are impressions and emotions received during the interaction. All the products are designed to be easy to use for their main purpose. For example, a plastic shopping bag is typically made thin and lightweight but strong even in contact with liquids, it usually has handles for comfortable carrying. The right and appropriate usage define at the time of product development. It has to provide a successful user experience and high usage frequency. After the development process, the original design idea of the product reaches the user and is interpreted by him personally. Human perception of the surrounding environment is usually different. Each user experience is formed in connection with particular needs, life activity, and a personâ€™s principles or values. Over the regular interaction, the user gets his own unique experience and collects information that allows him partially or completely to adopt the product to himself. Skilled user is able to rethink the original idea of design and ruin vision of designer in order to improve potential product usage. Clearly defined instruction or an excellent description of functionality cannot prevent the process of generating new knowledge while using the product. After the complex communication process with the user through an object, the designer let the concept live their own lives around the other intellections and mindset. The plastic shopping bag idea was to prevent chopping down the trees in the forests. The creator mainly believed that it would not be single-used but would be used every time again and again.

Originally the form of character was designed through drawings. Blender 2.8 software workspace was used to create a project. A three-dimensional creature was modeled to then set it in motion. An already existing armature set, Meta-Rig, was used.

The extra add-ons, "Rigging: Rigify" helps automate the creation of character rigs and bones with all the required controls systems. It also allows making an object move or shape change much easier and helps during the general animation process.
